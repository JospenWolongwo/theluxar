Frequently Asked Questions

How do I determine my ring size?

Our jewelry consultants can help determine your correct ring size during an in-store visit. Alternatively, we offer a printable ring sizer on our website, or you can schedule a virtual consultation with one of our experts who will guide you through the measurement process.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we offer personalized custom design services for special occasions such as engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our design team works closely with you to create a unique piece that reflects your personal style and preferences.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy from receipt of your order for unworn items in their original packaging. Custom-designed pieces and special orders are final sale. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to a defect or our error.
