Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are used in TheLuxar jewelry?

We use only premium materials in our jewelry, including 18k gold, sterling silver, and ethically-sourced gemstones. Our pieces are crafted with African-inspired designs and meet international quality standards.

How do I book a personal styling consultation?

You can book a consultation online through our website, by phone at +237 670 47 26 33, or visit us directly at our Makepe, Douala location. Our team will be delighted to assist you.

What is included in the warranty for watches?

Our watches come with a comprehensive warranty that covers manufacturing defects and mechanical issues. The warranty period varies by collection, ranging from 2 to 5 years, with extended options available.

Do you offer loyalty programs or special discounts?

Yes, we offer a VIP loyalty program for our regular customers with exclusive benefits, special previews of new collections, and personalized offers. Please ask our sales associates for more information.
